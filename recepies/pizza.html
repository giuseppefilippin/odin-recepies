Pizza

Pizza is a versatile and universally loved dish that combines a crispy or chewy crust with rich tomato sauce, melted cheese, and an array of toppings to suit any taste. Whether baked in a traditional oven or cooked on a pizza stone, this dish is perfect for casual dinners, parties, or family gatherings. With endless topping combinations, from classic pepperoni to veggie-packed options, pizza is a customizable and delicious favorite for all.

ingredients

  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, or other toppings of choice

steps

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes until frothy. Add flour, salt, and olive oil, mixing until a dough forms. Knead for 8-10 minutes until smooth. Cover and let rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.
  2. Assemble the Pizza: Roll out the dough on a floured surface into your desired shape. Transfer to a baking sheet or pizza stone. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust. Add shredded mozzarella cheese and your chosen toppings.
  3. Bake and Serve: Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C). Bake the pizza for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly. Remove from the oven, slice, and serve hot. Enjoy!
